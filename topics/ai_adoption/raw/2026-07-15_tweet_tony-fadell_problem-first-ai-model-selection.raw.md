## Source summary

Fadell rejects the idea that one foundation model will dominate every use case. He argues for selecting technology after defining what is being built and which experience it must deliver.

The linked TechCrunch article examines the growing production role of open-weight and private models, the economics of owning versus renting core AI capabilities, and the strategic risks of relying on one provider.
