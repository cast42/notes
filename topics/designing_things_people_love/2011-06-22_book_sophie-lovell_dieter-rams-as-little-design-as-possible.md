# Dieter Rams: As Little Design as Possible (Sophie Lovell)

## TL;DR
A core reference on Dieter Rams’ design philosophy and product work, centered on his “less, but better” mindset and ten principles of good design.

## Why it matters
- Connects Rams’ product language to practical design principles.
- Useful anchor for modern product/UI design decisions: clarity, honesty, durability, restraint.
- Frames design as long-term utility, not trend-following.

## Dieter Rams — 10 principles of good design
1. Good design is innovative.
2. Good design makes a product useful.
3. Good design is aesthetic.
4. Good design makes a product understandable.
5. Good design is unobtrusive.
6. Good design is honest.
7. Good design is long-lasting.
8. Good design is thorough down to the last detail.
9. Good design is environmentally friendly.
10. Good design is as little design as possible.
