# Become an Octopus Organization (HBR)

## TL;DR
A practical reminder for organizational change:
- make change *with* people, not *to* them,
- connect learning directly to impact,
- and reduce complexity so less effort creates more results.

## Key messages
- **Make changes with people, not to them.**
- **Entwine learning and impact.**
- **Do less to achieve more.**
