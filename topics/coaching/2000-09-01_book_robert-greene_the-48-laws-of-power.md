# The 48 Laws of Power (Robert Greene)

## TL;DR
A strategic handbook on power dynamics, influence, and self-protection in competitive environments.

## Why it matters
- Gives a vocabulary for reading power games and political behavior.
- Useful as a defensive lens: how influence is applied, signaled, and resisted.
- Often paired with ethical reflection to avoid manipulative overreach.
