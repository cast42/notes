## TL;DR
Avoiding unforced errors beats trying to look brilliant. Remove fatal mistakes from decisions, and long-term compounding does most of the heavy lifting.

## Key takeaways
- Prefer simple, low-risk moves over flashy high-risk ones.
- Invert decisions: ask what could ruin outcomes, then avoid that.
- Know your recurring cognitive biases and design around them.
- Consistency + error-avoidance compounds.
