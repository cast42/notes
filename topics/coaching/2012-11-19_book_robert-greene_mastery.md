# Mastery (Robert Greene)

## TL;DR
Greene’s framework for long-term excellence: find your life’s task, commit to apprenticeship, and build rare skills through deliberate practice.

## Why it matters
- Strong lens for career design and skill compounding.
- Emphasizes process, patience, and pattern recognition over shortcuts.
- Useful coaching reference for translating talent into sustained performance.
