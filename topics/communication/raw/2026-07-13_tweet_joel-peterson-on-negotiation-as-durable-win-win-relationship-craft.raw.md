Source summary provided by user:
- Negotiation is a conversation for durable win-win deals built on trust and relationships, not zero-sum wins or manipulation.
- Never show desperation — know your BATNA, stay patient, and use silence as a powerful tool.
- Separate people from the problem: focus on interests, not positions, and invent options for mutual gain.
- Negotiate directly with high-character, competent people who have decision power; be transparent, likable, and principled.
- View deals as serial (long-term reputation matters) — aim for creative solutions that build trust and avoid high-pressure tactics.
